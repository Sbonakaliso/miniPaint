Heads up! AndAttend is only for extra-classes, you still have to attend normal school.

Introduction

On AndAttend you can attend classes from wherever you are, if you have access to the internet. AndAttend works on Android (Firefox and Chrome) and on Windows (Firefox and Chrome).

Why you might want to use AndAttend

If you want to:
  • improve your marks at school
  • learn from the comfort of your home
  • want to try new teachers
  • Any other reason

How to use AndAttend

You go to www.AndAttend.gq and select the Timetable option from the menu, find out which which lesson you can take on what day and time. On the Timetable click the "Attend!" button for the subject of your choice.
