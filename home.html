Welcome to The Tutorial Centre

This is an online tutorial platform for all subjects. After quick setup, you and your tutor/student will see the same paint document, where you can both draw and write. You can talk to each other by voice or text. To start a tutorial, click the TutorialRoom on the navigation bar.

After the page has loaded, you will then see a purple button Start TutorTool, click it. Wait a moment and a light blue menu will appear on the right. The on top is where you can edit your profile information like name, user color and profile picture (if you want to).

Use the to get a room URL that you will have to share with your counterpart (via email, IM or Facebook). If you need a tutor you can search for one using the Find a tutor on the navigation. You will find a detailed list of tutors you can choose from.

Attention Tutors If you are having problems signing up, please send an email containing your name, email address, cell number, facebook address and hourly rate to holaTTC@gmail.com. We will enter you manually into the system.
The Tutorial Centre only works in Chrome, Firefox and Opera
