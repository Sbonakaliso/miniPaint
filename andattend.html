Congratulations! You are one of the first people to discover &Attend. The app that allows you to attend classes from anywhere on your smartphone, tablet or PC. Tell your friends about your discovery!

How &Attend works

  • Press the red Start attending button to open the timetable
  • Use the timetable to see which lessons are available at what time
  • Go to the timetable and click on a lesson to attend it
  • There is a monthly fee of R20 to use the app (paid at the beginning of the month)
  • There are free lessons and there are paid lessons
  • Use the free lessons to explore different teachers and see which ones you prefer
  • Use the paid lessons to get more lessons from that teacher
  • The payment system will use airtime on your phone
