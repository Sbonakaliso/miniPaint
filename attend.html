Wherever you are

Let's face it, waking up early on a Saturday morning to prepare for school is tough. Here you don't have to. Just wake up before extra lessons, log in and start attending.
If your school doesn't organize extra-classes for you, no worries because have arranged them.
It might be raining heavily, you still have to attend extra lessons. On &Attend weather has no influence.
Some extra lessons take place at night, on &Attend you don't have to risk your safety.

Teachers from anywhere

FACT: You prefare some teachers over others, too bad you don't choose you teacher at school. But wait, here you can try out and choose different teachers. This is important because students learn differently and teachers have different teaching styles.
Teachers from all over the country can teach on &Attend therefore you can access more teachers than you can wish for.

Save money

Traveling to school costs money, this may be transport but remember time is money too. What about the emotional distress of walking on a cold June Saturday morning, how much attension will you pay in class while breathing into and rubbing you hands together? Traveling costs more than money. Don't travel when you don't have to.
