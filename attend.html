Attend Classes from Anywhere...

...and improve your performance at school by attending free classes led by top South African teachers from your smartphone, tablet or PC.

Improve my marks!

Hello there, I am Sbo and 2 years ago I, too, was a student; so trust me when I say "I know what it feels like." I understand that in order to make it you need great teachers, that is why I have brought together teachers from the very best schools in Mzansi to &Attend. I also understand that as learners we prefer different teachers hence &Attend allows you to try different teachers out and pick the ones you prefer.

Learning is an amazing journey, experience it on this amazing platform. Learning takes place on our Virtual Classrooms, where you, the teachers and other students attending will see the exact same page. You and every other student will see what the teacher types live on your devices and you can also edit it. You will be able to speak with (and hear) the teacher as well as other students on the same Virtual Classroom.
